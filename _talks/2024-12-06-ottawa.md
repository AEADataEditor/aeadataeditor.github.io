## Abstract

The presentation discusses best practices for creating reproducible research when working with confidential data. It discusses what the core ingredients of a good README are, as well as licensing of data and code from the perspective of a public institution.

*Marie Connolly is Data Editor for the Canadian Journal of Economics. The session was jointly sponsored by the AEA and the Econometric Society.*
